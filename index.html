Practice

I have had a private practice in Boulder for 23 years. I offer medication management, psychotherapy, EMDR and hypnotherapy.

I work with clients reffered by their therapists for medication evaluations and specific trauma treatments.I also conduct therapy myself. I have a special interest in trauma, but I am comfortable working with most areas of general psychiatry

  • Depression
  • Anxiety Disorders including Panic Disorder
  • Bipolar Mood Disorder

I also specialize in:

  • Obsessive Compulsive Disorder
  • Trauma-related Disorders
  • Sleep Disorders
  • Existential/Spiritual Issues
  • Psychological problems associated with Traumatic Brain Injury
  • Psychological aspects of medical illnesses such as Cancer and Terminal Illness
  • Personality Disorders

About me

I am a Colorado native and i have been serving the Boulder community for over 28 years. Begining at Boulder County Mental Health and The Wardenburg Student Health Clinic and continuing with my own practice. I have established deep roots in Boulder County

I help Patients develop a plan for healing based on a combination of psychotherapy, Behavorial Management and Medication Management.

Listening and developing a relationship is an integral part of the healing process. I make myself available to my clients to be proactive to address problems as they arrive.
